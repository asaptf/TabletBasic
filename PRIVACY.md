# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

TabletBasic does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

TabletBasic does not collect any personal information, analytics, diagnostics,
tracking identifiers, usage history, source code, documents, or other user data.

## Network Access

TabletBasic does not communicate with the internet. The app does not contact
TabletBasic servers, third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or
any other external service.

## Files And Programs

Programs you write in TabletBasic remain on your device unless you choose to
save, export, copy, or share them using system features provided by Apple.

If you choose to store files in iCloud Drive or another file provider, any sync
or transfer is handled by that provider and by the operating system. TabletBasic
does not send your files to its own servers, because TabletBasic does not operate
any servers for user data.

## Third-Party Tracking

TabletBasic does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party
tracking technologies.

## Children

TabletBasic is designed as a programming learning tool. It does not knowingly
collect data from children or adults.

## Changes

If TabletBasic's privacy behavior changes in the future, this policy will be
updated before those changes are released.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the project maintainer through
the TabletBasic repository:

https://github.com/asaptf/TabletBasic
